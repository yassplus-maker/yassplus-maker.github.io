Brief & Goals

Medmar Agency manages the digital presence of multiple client brands simultaneously. I was responsible for the complete content output of four separate accounts — each with its own visual identity, tone, and language market.

Core goal: 20+ high-quality, on-brand visual assets per week across four distinct Instagram channels with zero missed deadlines across the full three-month placement.

What I Delivered

  • Produced and published 20+ original visual assets per week across 4 distinct Instagram brand pages, maintaining a separate visual identity and tone for each
  • Wrote and edited all captions and promotional copy in Arabic, English, and French, ensuring cultural relevance across 3 language markets simultaneously
  • Scripted, filmed, edited, and delivered short-form promotional video montages end-to-end for digital campaigns
  • Contributed to UI/UX wireframing and responsive web layout design using Figma and HTML/CSS, supporting 2 active agency web projects
  • Built and managed weekly content calendars across 4 brand accounts — zero missed publishing deadlines

Challenges

Maintaining four completely distinct visual identities without cross-contamination of style — a gold security-brand door retailer, a light editorial juice café, a bold media agency — each entirely different.

Copywriting in Arabic, English, and French each requires culturally specific idioms — different persuasive structures, register, and visual layout considerations given Arabic's right-to-left direction.
